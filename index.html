Beauty Tips
From Our Team

  • Moisturise daily — Keep your skin soft and hydrated morning and night.
  • Exfoliate weekly — Use a face scrub 1–2 times per week to remove dead skin.
  • Clean your brushes — Wash makeup brushes regularly to prevent breakouts.
  • Drink water — Hydration is the secret to naturally glowing skin.
  • Wear SPF — Always protect your skin from the Botswana sun.
